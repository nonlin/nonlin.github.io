George Erfesoglou - Director of Software | Unity/Unreal SDKs

Hands-on platform and engine-integration engineer building developer-facing SDKs and plugins for real-time 3D applications. Work centers on Unity and Unreal native/managed interop, BLE and serial transports, and reliability-focused device messaging pipelines.

Core Expertise

  • Interop and native boundaries: P/Invoke, C ABI design, Win32 serial overlapped I/O, WinRT, CoreBluetooth

  • Reliability engineering: framing/parsing, response correlation, timeouts/retries, lifecycle-safe teardown and diagnostics

Mustard soup (called Miyeouck-guck in Korea)

Ingredients

·         Beef

·         Sesame Oil

·         Mustard seed

·         Salt

Method

1.       Fry beef with sesame oil.

2.       Pour water to the pot where the beef is fried with sesame oil.

3.       If the water is boiling, put a seed mustard and salt.

4.       If the water is boiling again, it is finished!

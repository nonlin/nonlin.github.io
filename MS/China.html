Fry Shredded Potatoes

Ingredients

·         Potatoes

·         Green Peppers

·         Oil

·         Salt

Method

1.       Wash the potatoes and cut off the skins and then shred them.

2.       Put the shred potatoes into water and soak it for 10 minutes.

3.       Wash and shred green peppers.

4.       Heat the pot and put some oil into the pot.

5.       Wait for the oil become hot, then put the shredded potatoes and green peppers in to the pot. Keep stirring up and down until the potatoes become soft (which takes 1-2 minutes).

6.       Add some salt and stir a little bit. Put everything in the pot to the plate and then eat it.

Egg fried w/ tomato

Ingredients

·         Eggs

·         Tomatoes

·         Olive oil

·         Salt

·         Sugar

Method

1.       Pour eggs into a bowl and whip, and have the tomatoes cut

2.       Fry the eggs with olive oil.

3.       Fry the tomatoes in another pot until they turn soft.

4.       Put the fried eggs into the tomatoes and add salt and sugar

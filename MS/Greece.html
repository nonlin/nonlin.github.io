Spanakopita

Ingredients

·         3 Tablespoons olive oil

·         1 large onion

·         1 bunch of green onions (chopped)

·         2 cloves garlic (minced)

·         2 pounds spinach, rinsed and chopped

·         ½ Cup chopped fresh parsley

·         2 eggs (lightly beaten)

·         1 Cup crumbled feta cheese

·         1 box Filo (phyllo) dough sheets

·         ¼ Cup Olive oil

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly oil a baking sheet.
  2. Heat 3 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute onion, green onions and garlic, until soft and lightly browned. Stir in spinach and parsley, and continue to saute until spinach is limp, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix together eggs and feta. Stir in spinach mixture. Take 2 layers of filo dough and fold in half, length wise, brush with butter or oil. Then take a heaping tablespoon full of spinach and cheese mixture and place on one end of folded filo dough. Proceed to fold filo with spinach mixture into triangles. Brush with egg wash mixture. Continue until there is no more spinach mixture.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes, until golden brown.

  1. Remove from oven and let them cool down before enjoying.

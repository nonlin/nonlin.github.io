Llapingachos (Potato Pancakes)

Ingredients

·         4lbs of Potatoes, peeled and cubed

·         Salt

·         1/2 cup white onion, finely chopped

·         2 cups (8 ounces), white cheese (mozzarella or Halloumi)

·         A little bit of Olive oil (for frying)

Method

Cook the potatoes in water and salt until the potatoes are very soft. Mash the potatoes until smooth. Add the onion, and salt to taste. Leave mixture to settle for 3 hours. Add the cheese directly to the mixture or make a cheese pocket. Form the llapingachos 2 cm thick and ~5 cm in diameter. Get oil very hot and fry the llapingachos till they are golden brown on both sides. Be careful turning over because the pancakes may break in half. As toppings: fried eggs, avocados, tomatoes, hot sauce

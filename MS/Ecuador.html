Fried Chicken

Ingredients

·         Chicken

·         Flour

·         Salt

·         Pepper

·         Milk

·         1 Egg

·         Garlic Powder

·         Oil

Method

1.       Lightly salt and pepper chicken pieces

2.       Dredge in flour mixture (flour mixture: flour, salt, pepper, and garlic powder all to taste)

3.       Dip in egg and milk mixture

4.       Dredge in flour again

5.       Cook on medium high in corn or vegetable oil (Cooked best in an iron skillet)

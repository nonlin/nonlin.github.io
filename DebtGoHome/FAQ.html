What is Debt.goHome?

Debt.goHome uses pooled mining of profitable cryptocurrencies to generate income and pay off student loan debt.
By running Debt.goHome application your computer will use its hardware (GPU) to mine for a cryptocurrency (ZCash) as part of the pool called Nanopool. The program is made to only mine when you are not using your computer and you can configure the idle time it takes to start mining.

Nanopool takes a 1% fee from everything your computer mines and Debt.goHome takes another 1% fee to sustain itself, the other 98% of your computers work goes back to the community to pay off student loan debt.

How do I get rid of my debt?

The less you use your PC the more your PC mines, the greater the odds of you and someone else getting their student load debt paid off.

The more your computer mines the higher its rank gets. This rank is used to determine 3 out of 10 in the pay queue. 4 spots are picked randomly and 3 based on registration order.

How do increase my chances of getting randomly picked?

Refer a friend! The first friend you refer gets you 1 extra ticket into the random drawing, second friend gets you 3 and every friend after that gets you 5 tickets into the drawing. Everyone gets 1 ticket on registration.

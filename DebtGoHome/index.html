Together We Can Make A Difference

Debt.goHome wields the power of cryptocurrency to collectively mine and award miners real cash awards to help pay off student loan debt. Simply signup, download the application, run it and that's it! When you are not using your computer Debt.goHome will run in the background using your GPU to mine Ethereum. This Ethereum gets turned into real money to pay off real debt. Just 1% of your computers work is used to keep Debt.goHome alive.

Great! But How!

Upon signup and download Debt.goHome will keep track of how much mining your computer does. The more you mine the greater the chance you have of getting your loan funded, up to $50,000 per person. How fast your computer mines is up to your graphics card, the better the graphics card the more work your computer can do to mine your debt away.

No Worries

Debt.goHome is safe and here for you. If you don't have a top of the line computer, don't worry. Debt.goHome has a queue of 10 people, four spots are randomly selected, three spots are based on registration order and three spots resrerved for the top miners. All 10 people in queue get portions of their debt paid off, once each debts goal is met, we pay it off and send the debt back where it came from.
